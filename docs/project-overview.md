## Project Purpose

This project will create a professional author website for **Eric Deloach**, author of the *Rage of Angels* universe and *In Harm’s Way*.

The site will serve as the central online hub for Eric’s author brand, books, trailers, social presence, reader pathways, and retail links.

The website should help visitors quickly understand:

1. Who Eric Deloach is
2. What types of stories he writes
3. Which book or series they should start with
4. Where to buy or listen to his books
5. Where to watch trailers, shorts, and promotional media
6. How to follow or connect with him online

## Primary Website Goal

The main goal is to build a polished, cinematic author platform that supports book discovery, reader engagement, search visibility, and conversion.

The site should make Eric Deloach feel like a serious genre author with a clear creative identity.

## Author Positioning

Eric Deloach writes dark fantasy, crime, science fiction, thrillers, and gritty action stories where power, loyalty, vengeance, redemption, and humanity collide.

* Awards
* Reviews
* Bestseller claims
* Sales numbers
* Release dates
* ISBNs
* Retail availability
* Audiobook data
* Page counts
* Character names
* Character roles
* Author quotes
* Biographical claims
* Media coverage
* Reader testimonials

If a fact is not verified or client-approved, omit it or mark it as needing verification in an internal project note.
